EPIC Checklist for New Hotels
New Hotel On-boarding

Marriott's Enterprise Property Information Center (EPIC) is a centralized database that collects, stores, and distributes property information for all Marriott hotels. EPIC houses approximately 3,500 fields of data that are currently used by 46 internal and external systems.

While not all fields in EPIC can be viewed publicly each field of data in your property profile serves a critical purpose in selling and marketing your hotel. This document is a basic checklist outlining key EPIC action items for new properties on-boarding.

GM/EPIC Content Owner

Overview/Action
1. Complete Phase 1 Packet (PDF)

Applicable data loaded in your Phase 1 packet will be loaded in your EPIC property profile by our EPIC Support Team.

2. Register for EPIC Access

Each hotel should have a designated person (content owner) in charge of maintaining EPIC. In some cases, this may be a regional person.

3. Update/Customize Sales Descriptions and Highlights

Update the following message and highlight screens in EPIC under the Property Information/General Property Information/Sales Messages section:

  • Sales Descriptions, Guest Room Highlights, Local Area Highlights, Top Selling Features, Business/Group Highlights and Social Events/Meeting Highlights

NOTE: If you are working with an SEO provide, please consult with them prior to making changes in your Sales Messages

4. Update critical amenity sections in EPIC

Update the following amenity screens in EPIC under Property Information/Services & Amenities/:

  • Room Amenities, Guest Services, Food Services & Meal Plans, and Internet Access

5. Update critical fields related to property policies, payments & fees in EPIC

Update the following hotel policy screens in EPIC under Property Information/Reservations & Fees:

  • Hotel Policies and Payment & Fees

6. Update critical fields related to on-site facilities and area information in EPIC

Update the follow Area Information screens in EPIC under Property Information/Area Information:

  • Airport Information, Attractions, City Downtown, City Events and City Center

Update the following facility sections in EPIC under Property Information/Facilities/:

  • Hotel & Lobby Facilities, Restaurants and Guest Parking

7. Update critical fields related to compliance and safety and environmental programs.




 

Update the follow Area Information screens in EPIC under Property Information/Compliance & Safety:

  • Accessibility and Guest Room Security, Hotel Security, Emergency Assistance/Safety Compliance and Fire Safety & Prevention

Update the following facility sections in EPIC under Property Information/Environmental Programs:

  • Environmental Programs & Certifications, Certification, Clean Air, Energy Management, Solid Waste, Supplies, Green Hotel Global

8. Review EPIC Scorecard

MGS » Sales, Marketing & Revenue Management » eCommerce » EPIC » EPIC Scorecard

Review our self-evaluation tool highlighting critical data elements quarterly. Your hotel's percentage score will appear along with a green or red color code. If your hotel is red that means some of the fields were left blank (i.e., 'Please Select a Value') in EPIC.

*Note-Scores will be updated bi-weekly and fields will be changed quarterly.*

9. Review EPIC Lodging Quality Assurance Report

Property Reports-> EPIC LQA Reports-> Select year (XXXX) LQA Report

Review and update data fields listed in the annual LQA report to assure compliance with the EPIC portion of LQA audit. The LQA report Includes fields selected by EPIC team based on brand standards and/or feedback from systems receiving EPIC data. Fields requiring attention indicate “Please Select A Value” for your property response.

10. Review Property Reports

 Manage updates with the following reports:
  • Property Reports-> Property ProfileProperty Reports-> Expiring City Events
Identify fields used to support key initiatives with the following reports:
  • Property Reports-> ADA Report Property Reports-> Expanded Room Description Report Property Reports-> Property Internet Report (HSIA) Property Reports-> EPIC Fee Fields Report
Identify downstream systems fields with the following reports:
  • Property Reports-> PURE Fields Report Property Reports-> Marriott.com Fields Report
Identify EPIC new fields by year with the following reports:
  • Property Reports-> EPIC New Fields

11. Review Additional Resources on MGS

 HWS User Guide
Details the source of each section on your HWS page. The guide also contains instructions how to update content and images.

EPIC Critical Fields for MarRFP
Reviews critical fields used to pre-populate fields MarRFP. The fields pulled from EPIC are used to complete Account Proposals (RFPs) for Business Travel (BT) and data sent to Travel Management Companies (TMCs) for additional selling tools

EPIC Contact Sheet
 Identifies contacts for each of systems utilizing EPIC data and complete listing of fields you update in EPIC.

EPIC Events & Communications
Highlights upcoming EPIC events and communications.

Appendix A

The following section list fields in EPIC which may not be required for all properties. Depending on your property’s business composition, special amenity offerings or need to present guest with essential information, some or all of these fields may require attention or become critical data elements which may need to be completed prior to “go-live”.

Hotel Alerts Property Information/General Property Information/Alert & Renovations/Hotel Alert Hotel Alerts appear during reservation path and can be useful for getting information in front of guests before finalizing reservation.
Property Message Property Information/General Property Information/Sales Messages/Property Messages   Messages entered here will display on your property's Hotel Web Site and/or your eInterface Internet Portal. Property Messages are a good option to fit important or unique information that does not get captured elsewhere in EPIC.
Renovations Property Information/General Property Information/Alert & Renovations/Renovations   Used to capture active or upcoming renovations.
Meeting Rooms Property Information/Meetings & Events/Meeting Rooms   Key fields which captures data about meeting rooms offered and corresponding configurations
Meeting Facilities Property Information/Meetings & Events/Meeting Facilities Fields used to capture Information on property meeting facilities.
Spa Services Property Information/Services & Amenities/Spa Services Used to capture information on spa on-site or nearby.
Sales Tips and Business Mix, Value Proposition, Seasonality     Property Information/Global Sales Team Information/Renovation Details   Fields used to equip sales associates with the knowledge to promote your hotel.
Reservation eMail Hotel Messages Property Information/General Property Information/Sales Messages/Reservation eMail Hotel Messages   These fields allow property level messaging to appear on Confirmation, Redemption and Pre-Arrival emails.
Room Service Property Information/Services & Amenities/Food & Beverage Services/Room Service   Fields used to capture the type and hours of room service available.
Guest Parking Property Information/Facilities/Guest Parking Fields use to capture available parking options nearby or onsite.
Swimming Pools Property Information/Facilities/Pools/Swimming Pool Fields used to capture onsite pools
Fitness Facilities Property Information/Area Information/Recreation/Fitness Facilities Fields used to capture fitness facilities on-site or nearby.
Golf Courses & Golf Facilities Property Information/Area Information/Recreation/Golf Courses Property Information/Area Information/Recreation/Golf Facilities
Fields used to capture golf courses/facilities on-site or nearby.

To ensure your EPIC property profile is Complete, Correct, Current, utilize the following helpful tips:

  • Check EPIC regularly (e.g., once a quarter) or as information at the hotel changes (e.g., the pool is temporarily closed).

  • Customize highlights and sales messages in free form text fields and avoid duplicating service or amenity information collected elsewhere in EPIC. (Try to steer clear of Marriott/industry jargon.)

  • Avoid grammatical errors and use the SPELL CHECK functionality located at the bottom of the screen.

  • Complete all fields with a list of options- answering no, not available or not applicable instead of leaving a field blank.

  • Verify all data fields applicable to your hotel has detailed information, such as:
    • airports near hotel
    • facilities / services / features including room amenities, pools, dining, and Internet access.
